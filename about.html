Our story

It started with one family's need for better snacks.

From family recipe to community favorite.

When the founder's oldest son was diagnosed with dairy and gluten allergies, many available alternatives felt heavily processed and overly sweet. That sparked years of experimenting with gluten-free and dairy-free ingredients.

Granola became the family's favorite: eaten straight from the tray, poured as cereal, scattered over smoothie bowls, and even used as a salad topping.

Today, Dano's continues that same spirit—small batches, organic whole-food ingredients, natural sweetness, and a serious commitment to crunch.
